How it works

From records to
clinical intelligence

Upload medical documents, let AI extract and organise the data, then generate expert health reports — all in one platform.

Step 1

Upload Records

Upload lab reports, clinical documents, and medical PDFs. Our parser automatically extracts biomarkers, diagnoses, procedures, and genetic findings — no manual data entry required.

Step 2

AI-Powered Extraction

Structured clinical data is automatically organised into biomarkers, diagnoses, procedures, and genetics — with historical trends, reference ranges, and severity scoring built in.

Step 3

Generate Reports

Generate AI-powered health reports tailored to each patient — from comprehensive clinical analysis to biomarker trend tracking — surfacing the insights that matter most.

Security & Compliance

Built for clinical-grade trust.

Your patients' data demands the highest standards. We treat security not as a checkbox but as a foundational design principle.

HIPAA Compliant

Full administrative, physical, and technical safeguards

Multi-Factor Authentication

TOTP, hardware keys, and backup codes built in

Encrypted at Rest & In Transit

XChaCha20-Poly1305 at rest, TLS in transit

Audit Logging

Every authentication event and data access logged with full trail

Clinician Control

Every AI-generated output is fully editable. Nothing reaches a patient without explicit clinician sign-off — your expertise is always the final authority.

Access Controls

Role-based access with automatic session timeouts. Multi-factor authentication available. Patient data is scoped to authorised clinicians only.

Data Sovereignty

Your data stays yours. We never sell patient information. Records are stored in encrypted cloud infrastructure with signed, time-limited access URLs.
